The Lima Major 2023
The Lima Major 2023 is the first Major of the 2023 Dota Pro Circuit season. It is the first ever South American DPC Major.
Team Division I Qualified
Beastcoast - South America Team Division I
Evil Geniuses - South America Team Division I

South America is going to host a Major for the very first time in the storied history of Dota 2 esports. After repeatedly putting up notable displays at various Majors and TIs, South America has cemented itself as a growing region in Dota 2 esports.
